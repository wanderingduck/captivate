About

Create Captivate was founded by Michael Kirk an experienced digital marketer and designer from Leicester, UK. Michael has worked with businesses such as John Lewis, Waitrose, Morrisons and numerous SMEs over the last few years and is experienced in both B-2-B and B-2-C marketing.

In order to offer a seamless business marketing service, Michael works alongside other trusted specialists such as web developers, printers, videographers, and copywriters.

Create Captivate offers a complete service covering business brand concept, logo creation, website design, promotional videos, organic search (SEO) and pay per click (PPC) campaign management. Create Captivate will get your business off the ground at a cost that suits your budget and train you and your team to manage the process in the future.

Services

Brand

Your brand is far more than just a logo. It's your whole customer experience. Brand is your website, your social media experience, the way you answer the phone, it's what your customer tells others about your company.

Web

Your website should be beautiful, but more importantly it should be functional and easy to use, while contributing to your overall brand experience. Keep things simple, user friendly and most importantly plan it well.

Print

These days many businesses are opting purely for digital marketing as it is perceived as more cost effective and while this may be true to some respects, high quality print such as brochures and Letterheads say a lot about the quality of your business.

SEO/PPC

Getting your website found on Google is essential for many businesses and can minimise the need for sales, but SEO can be costly. Thats why you need to hire an agency that will teach you (from scratch) how to manage your own SEO and PPC campaigns.
